Our Story

Built around one fire.

The Olive & Oak started as a single wood-fired hearth and a belief that the best meals come from cooking simply with what's good that day.

Since Day One

A kitchen led by the season

We opened The Olive & Oak with a simple rule: nothing on the menu that couldn't be traced back to a farm, a boat, or a forager we know by name. That rule still holds. Our menu is rewritten as the market changes — not the other way around.

The dining room was designed to slow people down: low light, close tables, a bar built for lingering over one more glass.
